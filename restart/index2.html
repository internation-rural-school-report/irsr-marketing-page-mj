Information for Schools

Is your school in need of equipment management? Are you having difficulties getting the supplies you need to do your job effectively? Do you need an easy way to keep all stakeholders informed about the conditions of your school? International Rural School Report is the tool for you!

International Rural School Report offers school administrators a way to report on the conditions of equipment at their schools. Did a projector break? Do your students need new books? It's all reportable in an easy and convenient format. All you need is internet connection.

We have two different school log in types depending on your designated role within the school:

School Administrators

School administrators can create, read, update and delete information on the condition of the school, including general issues and the condition of equipment and other devices in the school.

Board Members

Board members can schedule actions, mark requests or items as done, ignore items and comment with more information. They can also use the system to communicate with school administrators about equipment and supply-related needs.

Information for Donors

Do you care about education? Do you want to make sure rural schools have access to equipment they need to do their best work? International Rural School Report may be what you've been looking for.

International Rural School Report is designed to ensure that rural schools get access to what they actually need. This is possible because International Rural School Report is first and foremost an equipment management service for school administrators and board members to keep track of what equipment is good and working, what may be damaged and needs repair, and what's missing.

International Rural School Report now has the ability for you -- a person who cares deeply about education and wants to help rural schools succeed -- to connect with the actual school administrators and board members about what they actually need.

Nothing more. Nothing less.
