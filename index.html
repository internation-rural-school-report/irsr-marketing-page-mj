Information for Schools

Is your school in need of equipment management? Are you having difficulties getting the supplies you need to do your job effectively? Do you need an easy way to keep all stakeholders informed about the conditions of your school? International Rural School Report is the tool for you!

International Rural School Report offers school administrators a way to report on the conditions of equipment at their schools. Did a projector break? Do your students need new books? It's all reportable in an easy and convenient format. All you need is internet connection.

We have 2 different school log in types depending on your role within the school.

  • School Administrators
  • Board Members
