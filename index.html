What we do

The Basics

With International Rural School Report, you can streamline the way your school manages facilities, equipment and supplies.

Because everything is online and easily accessible to school administrators and board members, schools can stay in communication and organized about maintenance issues and supply needs.

User Roles

School Administrators

School administrators can create, read, update and delete reports on conditions in the school, including facility maintenance issues, repair needs, and supply requirements.

Board Members

Board members can schedule actions for school administrators, mark reports as done, ignore reports and use the system for communication regarding specific issues.

Connect from anywhere

International Rural School Report makes it simple to communicate with everyone involved with maintenance and facilities at your school, no matter where they are, with the click of a button.

How it works

International Rural School Report is a centralized, online management system for school facilities designed with rural schools in mind.

Reports

International Rural School Report gives users the ability to report issues and organize them by date.

Issue Categories

  • security
  • electrical
  • textbooks
  • school supplies
  • tech equipment

How to Report

Users with school administrator level access can make a report with the date, the issue category, and any relevant information or images. The app keeps track of who makes which reports and updates in real time.

View Formats

You can view issues in summary or as single issues with more detailed information. The status report function allows you to keep track of what has been resolved, what is in progress, and what needs to be done. Users with board member level access can update the status of issues and mark things as resolved, ignored, or pending for peace of mind.

There is also the option to review and update resolved issues, including the original issue report, who resolved it, and how it was resolved. This gives schools the ability to keep track of past actions and record maintenance and inventory management in real time across multiple devices.
