What we do

The Basics

With International Rural School Report, you can streamline the way your school manages facilities, equipment and supplies.

Because everything is online and easily accessible to school administrators and board members, schools can stay in communication and organized about maintenance issues and supply needs.

We support two different roles in the app:

School Administrators

School administrators can create, read, update and delete reports on conditions in the school, including facility maintenance issues, repair needs, and supply requirements.

Board Members

Board members can schedule actions for school administrators, mark reports as done, ignore reports and use the system for communication regarding specific issues.

International Rural School Report makes it simple to communicate with everyone involved with maintenance and facilities at your school, no matter where they are, with the click of a button.
