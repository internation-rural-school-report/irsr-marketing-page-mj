Information for Schools

Keep tabs on everything.
Nothing is too small to track.

Is your school in need of equipment management?
Are you having difficulties getting the supplies you need to do your job effectively?
Do you need an easy way to keep all stakeholders informed about the conditions of your school?
International Rural School Report may be the tool for you!

What we do

International Rural School Report offers school administrators a streamlined way to report on the conditions of equipment at their schools in an easy and convenient format. All you need is internet connection.

You can keep track of:

  • Devices, like computers, tablets, HDMI cords, power cables, etc.
  • the condition of equipment, like furniture in need of repairs, etc.
  • supply quantities and needs
  • and much, much more!

We have two different school log in types depending on your designated role within the school:

School Administrators

School administrators can create, read, update and delete information on the condition of the school, including general issues and the condition of equipment and other devices in the school.

Board Members

Board members can schedule actions, mark requests or items as done, ignore items and comment with more information. They can also use the system to communicate with school administrators about equipment and supply-related needs.
